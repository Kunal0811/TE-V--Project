Submit Your Grievance

We are committed to addressing your concerns efficiently. Follow the instructions below to submit a grievance about community issues.

How to Submit a Grievance

  1. Provide Details: Clearly describe the issue you are facing. Include as much information as possible to help us understand and address the problem.
  2. Upload Supporting Documents: Attach any relevant documents or images that support your grievance. This helps in the investigation and resolution process.
  3. Submit Your Form: Review your information and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email with a unique grievance ID for tracking.

Submit Your Grievance

Tips for Effective Grievance Submission

  • Be Specific: Provide detailed information about the issue. Specifics help in understanding and addressing the problem more effectively.
  • Attach Evidence: Any photos, documents, or other evidence can greatly assist in the resolution process.
  • Keep Records: Save the confirmation email and grievance ID for future reference and tracking.
  • Follow Up: If you do not receive updates, feel free to follow up on your grievance status through our platform.
